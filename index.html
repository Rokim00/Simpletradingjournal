How to Use This Journal

Each day, log your trades. You may take a maximum of 2 trades/day. If your first trade is profitable, stop for the day. If it's a loss, you can take one more trade. Use a consistent lot size and a fixed risk-reward of 1:3.

For each trade, input the number of pips won or lost. Also input your lot size for the day. Your dollar profit will be calculated automatically using: (Pips / 10) Ã— (Lot Size / 0.01).

Use the "Traded Today" checkbox to log which days you participated. Skip days are also part of discipline!
